SMTP Conversation
This is SMTP Protocol. Do NOT type in "Send String" That is just notifying you for when you need to send a new bit of information. Sorry about the simplicity of this tutorial.




HELO 255.255.255.255
MAIL FROM: FROM@EMAIL.COM
RCPT TO: TO@EMAIL.COM
DATA
From: FROM@EMAIL.COM
To: TO@EMAIL.COM
Subject: SUBJECT
EMAIL MESSAGE
.
QUIT
